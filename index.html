About

The Center for eResearch is coordinating a week long training and education event designed to offer opportunities for all research students, faculty, and staff to up-skill in subjects related to digital research practice. By holding a number of different eResearch-related training events in the same week we aim to create additional buzz and 'hype' around the training than if the events were held separately.

We are inviting anyone who is interested in using the event to host their own workshops, introductions or other sessions to get in touch. The idea is to co-locate many training, awareness, or educational offerings from across the University into a coherent space and place that can be advertised or communicated under this one umbrella. The theme is broadly around 'research enablement' and 'digital research skills'.

Workshops or offerings are developed, organised, and run by their respective owners. We at CeR will help provide the space, and assist with communication and coordination of the week event as a whole.

Contact Us

If you would like to be involved or host a workshop or seminar for Winter Bootcamp, please contact Cameron (ca.mclean@auckland.ac.nz) or Sina (s.ansari@auckland.ac.nz) at the CeR.
